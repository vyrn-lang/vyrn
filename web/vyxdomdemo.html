Vyrn × vyrn-dom.js — .vyx components

examples/vyxdomdemo.vyrn composes its view() from .vyx components (RFC-0026 M4) over module state (RFC-0026). The wasm module exports vyrnView() / vyrnSubs() and one export extern fn per handler; vyrn-dom.js builds the DOM, delegates events by name, diffs after every handler (keyed lists preserve nodes), and reconciles the Every subscription by value.
